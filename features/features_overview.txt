
路view_movie_details.feature
  Feature: View movie details
  In order to view all the details about a certain movie
  As a user,
  I want to be provided the general information about a certain movie (release
  year, director, actors,...) and a source where I can watch/buy the movie and
  look at reviews.

路list_released_movies.feature
  Feature: List just released movies
  In order to keep myself up to date about latest movie releases,
  As a user,
  I want to view the last movie releases.

路pin_movie_as_seen.feature
  Feature:  Pin movie as seen
  In order to distinguish between seen and not seen films,
  As a user,
  I want to be able to pin a movie as already seen.

路review_movie.feature
  Feature: Review movie
  In order to share my opinion about a movie,
  As a user,
  I want to register, modify or delete my opinion on a certain movie by giving
  a rating and an optional comment.
